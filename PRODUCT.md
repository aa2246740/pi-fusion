# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Pi users, agent builders, open-source evaluators, and engineers comparing multi-model orchestration systems. They arrive from GitHub, npm, or the project website and need to decide quickly whether Pi Fusion is a real evaluated system or another unscored fusion demo.

## Product Purpose

Pi Fusion gives Pi agents a local model-fusion layer: independent participant models, judge synthesis, optional evidence tools, workspace sandboxes, and local artifacts. Public positioning should make the DRACO scored validation clear before feature depth, while keeping benchmark claims precise and defensible.

## Brand Personality

Measured, technical, evidence-first.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI landing pages that promise better answers without scored evidence. Avoid claims that imply official DRACO certification, universal superiority over Fusion API, or access to private benchmark artifacts during generation. Avoid decorative layouts that bury benchmark methodology below vague product language.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with scored proof, then explain the product.
2. Separate generation from scoring whenever benchmark claims appear.
3. Make numeric comparisons scannable with tables, metric rows, and short labels.
4. Keep public artifacts sanitized and avoid raw prompt, rubric, answer, or private scoring details.
5. Favor inspectable local artifacts over opaque claims.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve reduced-motion behavior. Make video optional with surrounding text summaries so the benchmark story remains understandable without playback.
